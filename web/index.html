What Your Rapid-Site Package Includes

I handle the entire stack—domain, DNS, hosting, security, forms, email routing and routine edits—so you never wrestle with registrars or cPanel. You keep 100 % ownership of all content. Cancel any time and I’ll package the files & transfer the domain if you’d rather self-host. Most clients simply renew once a year and let me keep everything fast, secure and SEO-friendly.

  • Hosting & management: The site lives on my GitHub Pages account behind Cloudflare’s edge network. I monitor uptime, performance and security.
  • Domain: Registered through my Namecheap account and renewed on your behalf. Transfer out any time—or let it lapse and re-register yourself.
  • DNS & security: Cloudflare SSL, caching and basic analytics (included).
  • Search & analytics: Google Search Console + Google Analytics under my workspace (viewer access for you) or linked to your own Google account.
  • Contact forms: Formspree under my paid workspace for spam-free submissions.
  • Email routing: MXRoute alias forwarding to your existing inbox. Extra mailboxes $5 / mo each.
  • Content updates: Two 30-minute text/image tweaks per month included. Larger changes billed at $75 / hr.
  • Back-ups & version control: GitHub repo history plus off-site snapshot each release.
  • Ownership: You own all copy, graphics and data. If you cancel, I’ll transfer files & DNS upon request.
  • Billing: Single annual invoice — $200 service + domain renewal + any mailbox add-ons (30-day grace period).
